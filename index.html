About Us

The second edition of Infinity an intra-college technical symposium is proudly presented by the Computational Sciences Association, Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences, PSG College of Technology on "13th, 14th and 15th February 2020". The symposium is filled with six exciting events for the first and second years of all B.E/B.Tech/B.Sc/M.Sc/MCA programmes.

Infinity brings the most significant platform for upbringing technical and non-technical skills of the first and second-year techies by providing them the key opportunity to showcase out-breaking talents in various events. We come up with events that are drafted to let out the team spirit, leadership capability and the desire to innovate.

Prozone

FOR EVERY BIT OF CODER IN YOU!

Just another coding contest, with some twists and turns. We have three rounds. Code through the first two rounds without actual coding and code as much as you want to in the third one.
A team can consist of at most two members.
Timing: 13th February 4:45pm to 5:30pm

Inkling

UNLOCK KNOWLEDGE AT THE SPEED OF THOUGHTS

Refresh your intelligence and work yourself on allusion where you get Technical and innovative questions that represents a simple answer.
Team should contain 2 members.
Timing: 13th February 5:30pm to 6:15pm

Incognito

BEAT THE CLOCK AND TEAR THE CLOAK

Incognito aims to instill a desire for analytical expertise, rational thinking, logical thought process and astute decision-making.Skim, scan, read, flip through and get at most out of google. Dive in as duo, together you can witness the venture.
Timing: 14th February 4:45pm to 5:45pm

Fuzzle-Buzzle

MAKE YOUR SKILLS WIN OVER THE RUNNING CLOCK!

Are you tired of codes and compilers? Here’s a completely fun-filled event for you! All you need is the urge to show you up. We test your spontaneity and how well you manage your time.
Timing: 15th February 9:00am to 12:30am

Gamoholix

MARK OF A PROFESSIONAL GAMER

It’s not gonna be easy, but it’s worth it.
PUBG - Team should contain 4 members.
NFS - Single player.
Timing: 15th February 1:30pm to 3:00pm

Tech Hunt

SCAN BETWEEN EVERY BLINK

All you have to do is code, but without typing. Hunt, Scan, and Chase all the chunks. Here your spontaneity matters more than your syntax.
One round, one winner. The fastest wins.
Timing: 15th February 10:30pm to 12:00pm
